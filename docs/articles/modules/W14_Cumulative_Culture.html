Welcome to the orientation module for PSYC 3043! We will cover everything here and more on the first day of class. See you there.

Goals

Instructions

Work through each of the following Read, Annotate, Engage, and Reflect sections. Reading and annotating is always due before class, whereas Engaging and Reflecting is due at the end of the week.
